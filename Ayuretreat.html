Ayurvedic Retreat in Nepal

Experience a transformative journey at our partner wellness centers in Nepal, blending authentic Panchakarma therapies, Ayurvedic massages, nourishing meals, and guided resets.

Retreat Highlights

  • Authentic Panchakarma detox therapies
  • Daily Ayurvedic massages and treatments
  • Personalized meal plans based on your dosha
  • Guided yoga, meditation, and mindfulness sessions
  • Nature walks and cultural immersion in Nepal
  • Expert Ayurvedic doctors and therapists

3–5 Day Program Overview

  • Day 1: Arrival, Ayurvedic consultation, gentle detox
  • Day 2: Panchakarma therapies, massage, yoga
  • Day 3: Deep cleansing, meditation, nature walk
  • Day 4: (Optional) Advanced therapies, cultural immersion
  • Day 5: Integration, closing rituals, departure

Who is it for?

This retreat is ideal for anyone seeking a holistic reset, relief from stress, or support for chronic health concerns. Perfect for individuals, couples, or small groups.
